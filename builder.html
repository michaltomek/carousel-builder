How to Create a Carousel in 5 Steps

1

Pick Your Font

Choose from 6 professional fonts (Inter, Playfair, Poppins, Montserrat, Lato, Roboto). Applied to all slides.

2

Add Your Text

Write your headline and subtext for each slide. Highlight specific words to make them stand out.

3

Customize Colors

Set headline color, subtext color, and background. Use color pickers or paste hex codes.

4

Add Branding

Upload your logo/headshot and handle. Position in corners or show only on the last slide.

5

Export & Share

Download as PDF (for LinkedIn) or PNG images (for Instagram). All changes auto-save.
